Clubbersise

Want to exercise but hate cliques, hard to learn routines, brightly lit rooms and boring music choices? Clubbercise is so much fun, youll forget its exercise!

Clubbercise classes are taught in a darkened room with disco lights and our trademark LED glow sticks set to a soundtrack of old and new club hits. A fusion of dance, toning and combat moves with high/low impact options for all fitness levels, one class burns around 600 calories.

Then we work up a sweat to club anthems from 90s classics to the latest chart hits. The routines are a mix of dance, toning and combat, they are easy-to-follow and always have high or low impact options to suit all fitness levels. Its all about fun, not about perfection. Take it to your level, keep it simple and enjoy the music.

A Better Night's Sleep

Sleep is as important to our wellbeing as nutrition and exercise; yet very few of us achieve the recommended amount.

Matt Williams from the Oxford Academic Health Science Network will talk about the importance of sleep and about a new, free-of-charge online programme called Sleepio which aids better sleep without pills or potions.

Chronic sleeplessness affects between one in five and one in ten adults of working age. Last year across England over 12 million prescriptions for insomnia were written, at a cost to the NHS of 72m one prescription for every GP, every day of the year. Sleepio is a web-based fully-automated, self-help programme based on cognitive behavioural therapy approaches. It is a proven alternative to sleeping pills, improving sleep in three out of four users. Find out why sleep matters and how we can all achieve a better nights sleep.
